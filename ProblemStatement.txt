#26ENHL3: Problem Statement 3
🏢 HireLoop — End-to-End Campus Recruitment Portal
The Challenge: Build a complete campus placement management system connecting
students, college placement cells, and recruiting companies — with AI-powered resume
analysis and mock interview features.
Core Features You Must Build:
Three User Roles
● Student: Profile, resume upload, job applications, interview preparation
● Recruiter (Company): Post jobs, view applicants, shortlist, schedule interviews
● Placement Cell (Admin): Manage all companies, approve job posts, view student
stats, generate reports
Student Side
● Build and download a resume directly from the platform (select template, fill
sections, export as PDF)
● AI Resume Analyser: upload resume → get ATS score, missing keywords,
improvement suggestions
● Apply to posted jobs with one click
● Track application status: Applied → Shortlisted → Interview Scheduled → Offer →
Rejected
● AI Mock Interview: role-specific questions, answer evaluation, session summary
Recruiter Side
● Post job openings: role, eligibility (CGPA, branch, batch), deadline, salary range
● View all applicants with filters (CGPA, branch, skills)
● Shortlist applicants and schedule interview slots
● Send automated email notifications on status changes
Placement Cell Side
● Approve or reject company registrations
● Dashboard: total students placed, companies registered, packages offered
● Generate placement report by branch, batch, or company (downloadable)
● Announcement board: post notices visible to all students
Payments
● Companies pay a listing fee to post jobs (sandbox checkout)
● Students can upgrade to Premium: AI interview unlimited + priority visibility to
recruiters
AI Features
● Resume scoring against a job description (match percentage)
● Auto-generate cover letter draft from resume + job description
● Smart job recommendations based on student skills and past applications
